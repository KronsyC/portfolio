# Introducing my new portfolio website

Welcome to my new portfolio website, you may have noticed that this new site utilizes the [Hugo](https://gohugo.io) static site building tool with [this theme](https://hugoloveit.com). This is because I am an entirely untalented frontend developer.

---

But wait, if this is the new one, what happened to the old one?

Glad you asked!

Although this is technically my first portfolio site, I have had many attempts in the past to make one, as you may be able to see from my github profile. However, my unfortunate phobia of frontend development has lead to all of my attempts dying after a couple of days of development.
So in order to actually have a working portfolio site for once, I have decided to bite the bullet, take the lazy-person's approach and just use a template.

There may come a time in the distant future where I can finally muster up enough courage to build (and finish) a portfolio website. But for now, this site will suffice just fine, and honestly looks much better than anything I could ever style up myself.
